About Me

I am a Northern Arizona University spring 2023 graduate with experience in the technology and entertainment industries.

For the past 7 years, I have owned a successful audiovisual productions company based in Southern California, which has established several high-profile clients. Before that, I worked as a software developer for 5 years, building full-stack web and mobile applications for large companies, consulting firms, early-stage startups, and mid-stage startups. At one point, I even co-founded my own tech startup in the automotive space. As its CTO, my role was to manage a team of developers based overseas. Although the startup ended up not working out, it provided me with a valuable opportunity to develop my leadership and management skills.

Currently, I am continuing to run my company. I also work part-time as a caregiver for the disabled and the elderly; caring for the disabled holds a special place in my heart because I have a younger sister with special needs. I also recently worked for the San Diego Latino Film Festival doing social media, marketing, and PR, which was a fun and meaningful pursuit.

Once I graduate from Northern Arizona University on May 12, 2023, I am seeking to relocate for a new position that will challenge me and allow me to implement my skills and experience.
