Liberty Mutual Express Estimate Mobile App


From 2015 to 2016, I worked as a software developer at AudaExplore, a Solera Company. AudaExplore is a leading provider of software for the automotive and claims processing industry. I worked on the mobile team, which was tasked with creating Android and iOS applications for car insurance companies. Notable among them was the Liberty Mutual Express Estimate app.

The Express Estimate app allows clients to file a claim, manage an existing claim, upload photos, request roadside assistance, and access ID cards.

During my first two weeks at AudaExplore, I was tasked with performing quality assurance testing on this app. Then, I immediately moved into working on the app's 3D damage viewer. The 3D damage viewer, which was coded in WebGL and three.js, simulated dents and scratches on the vehicle, as well as their depth using a heatmap. I was tasked with making the 3D damage viewer responsive using Bootstrap, adding undo/redo functionality to the dents and scratches, and developing the heatmap.
